Having been written off so many time and also writing himself off, learn how the heart of this man kep him alive and influenced/saved the lives of countless people.

Hero of Hero's

Each time he overcame one of these hardships he walked the a path that led him to a happier one. At the time he could not see it but each decision he made, and each action he took molded him into an impossibly good man, like no one has ever or will ever see again.
